About SAMADHAN

SAMADHAN is a team project for SIH 2023 aimed at providing a platform for citizens to report water leaks in their area. Our goal is to make it easier for people to report leaks, and to help ensure that they are repaired quickly and efficiently.

Benefits of Using SAMADHAN

We are aiming to solve non revenue water problem with our SAMADHAN ,a android and web based application to save water loss due to leakage or theft. The prototype is as follows:

  • Citizens Can Report Any Leakage Or Theft of water resources in their nearby areas through our platform.
  • This Report will be sent to nearby registered plumbers as Alert and immediately send location of the area
  • Plumbers will then reach the location with the help of our Geolocation feature
  • Once the problem gets fixed the information will be send to the Database and our Machine Learning model will gather this information for future forecast of any such accidents.
  • In contrary we will also use the Artificial Intelligence(AI) technology to identify the requirements of plumbers in different districts based on the reports lodged.
